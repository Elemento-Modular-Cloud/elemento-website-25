In recent times, an extraordinary opportunity has emerged for telecommunications companies to make a significant impact on reducing their ecological footprint, potentially achieving a remarkable 80% decrease by 2025. This endeavor involves harnessing the power of spatial data analysis, artificial intelligence, and machine learning to efficiently construct and manage networks while accurately estimating end-user demand.

The annual meeting of the World Economic Forum, which brings together political, economic, civil society, and media leaders, has placed particular emphasis on sustainability as a prominent theme.

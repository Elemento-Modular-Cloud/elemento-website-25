The new cloud for cultural heritage: digital transformation serving safeguarding

By giulia-borgoni — 2025-06-24

The creation of ....

The European Commission has recently launched an intriguing initiative for the safeguarding of cultural heritage: the creation of a collaborative cloud. This innovative digital infrastructure aims to foster transdisciplinary collaboration among experts in the field, enabling them to share knowledge, digitize artifacts, and promote research and study of artworks. With a budget of 110 million euros allocated by the Horizon EU project, the new cloud for cultural heritage aims to successfully address the challenges of digital transition, opening doors to a future full of opportunities.

A Bridge Across Disciplines:

At the heart of this initiative lies the digital infrastructure that facilitates collaboration among various professionals involved in cultural heritage preservation. Specialists such as scholars of cultural heritage, curators, archivists, and conservators can work synergistically, sharing knowledge, experiences, and resources through this innovative platform. This type of transdisciplinary collaboration represents a significant step forward in enhancing European cultural heritage.

Unlocking Untapped Potential:

One of the key outcomes of this new cloud for cultural heritage will be the unlocking of untapped potential in the sector. The platform will enable the organization of joint exhibitions, facilitating the sharing of artworks among different European cultural institutions. Furthermore, the digitization of artifacts will be a priority, allowing broader and easier access to cultural heritage. This will not only preserve works in the long term but also offer new research and study opportunities for experts.

European Cultural Heritage as a Global Leader:

The fundamental objective of the collaborative cloud for European cultural heritage is to position Europe as a global leader in implementing digital technologies in the cultural sector. Through this initiative, the aim is to create added value for European citizens by promoting accessibility, preservation, and understanding of cultural heritage. The collaborative cloud will provide a secure and scalable digital environment, enabling European cultural institutions to share their resources and expertise, thus creating synergies that will have a lasting impact in the field of heritage safeguarding.

The concept of multi-cloud often evokes dread: complex setups, confusing costs and operational headaches. However, sticking to a single provider has its downsides: vendor lock-ins, limited flexibility and the inability to leverage "best of breed" solutions.

At Elemento we’ve made multi-cloud not only possible but practical. Here’s how.

‍The common pitfalls of multi-cloudManaging multiple clouds is challenging for several reasons:

  • Operational complexity: configuring and maintaining several providers demands expertise and time.
  • Cost tracking: with different pricing models and hidden fees, managing costs across providers can feel like an unsolvable puzzle.
  • Data interoperability: transferring workloads between clouds is often cumbersome, requiring complex integrations.

Elemento’s multi-cloud solution

We believe multi-cloud doesn’t have to be a nightmare. With Elemento, you gain:

  1. Unified management: our no-code interface consolidates all your cloud environments into one cockpit.
  2. Cost transparency: real-time cost observability ensures you’re always in control of your budget.
  3. Price brokering: optimize your costs effortlessly by switching between providers based on the best deals available.
  4. Interoperability: with a common language bridging all clouds, you eliminate vendor lock-ins and simplify integrations.

Why choose multi-cloud?

A multi-cloud strategy offers unmatched flexibility and resilience. By spreading workloads across providers you minimize risks and gain access to a diverse set of tools and technologies. With Elemento multi-cloud management becomes intuitive, scalable and cost-effective.

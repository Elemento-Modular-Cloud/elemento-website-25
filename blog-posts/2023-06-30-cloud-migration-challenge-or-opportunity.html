The challenges of cloud migration

Let’s face it: the path to the cloud is complex.

Studies reveal:

  • Only 15% of applications transition effortlessly to the cloud.
  • 25% of apps require complete rewrites.
  • The majority (60%) demand significant refactoring.

These figures highlight the technical and strategic hurdles businesses face. Without the right partner cloud migration risks becoming a costly, time-consuming endeavor.

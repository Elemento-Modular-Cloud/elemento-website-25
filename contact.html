Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get started?

You can start using Elemento immediately with our free trial. Most customers are up and running within 30 minutes.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer comprehensive support including documentation, community forums, email support, and priority support for enterprise customers.

Can I deploy on-premises?

Yes! Elemento is designed to be self-hostable, giving you complete control over your infrastructure and data.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Absolutely. Our enterprise team works closely with customers to develop custom solutions that meet specific requirements.

What compliance standards do you support?

We support SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and other major compliance frameworks with built-in security features.

How do you handle data privacy?

Data privacy is built into our platform. You control where your data is stored and processed, with encryption at rest and in transit.
